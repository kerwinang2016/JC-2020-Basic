<p>Total account balance can be viewed anytime under the billing section of your ordering system. The balance shown is the total
amount of open invoices, not invoices which are due for payment.</p><br/>

<p>Upon customer orders being processed and accepted, all invoicing is automatically generated within 24 hours under the billing
section of your ordering system. Invoices are generated with a due date noted, of which the system automatically turns the due
date <span style="color:#d3232b;font-weight:bold;">RED</span> when invoices become outstanding.</p><br/>

<p>Invoices can be located in the ordering system by navigating:</p>
<i>My Account > Billing > Invoices.</i><br/><br/>
<small>Note: Invoices are not automatically emailed but can be downloaded via the billing section of your ordering system.</small>
